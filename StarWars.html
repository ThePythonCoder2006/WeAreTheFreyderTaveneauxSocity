STOPPING  WEAPON  TRAFFICKING

All around the world 500 000 people get killed by weapon that no one knew about, that were not regulated

Very few countries try (and fail) to regulate non registerd weapons. This is because they used the wrong method.

They only added restriction to LEGAL firearms and never even imagined that this would only make the black market bigger and juicier for weapon sellers.

It is henceforth needed to find new and better solutions and moreover to apply these solutions...
